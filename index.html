AI · Client work · Proof of concept

VoiceLog

Spotted the need on-site. Built the MVP solo. Client took it forward.

Electricians are often behind on filling in reports on jobs they perform. Late reports are more prone to errors and cause problems for invoicing. It's also a task electricians don't enjoy doing. I identified this problem at a meeting with the client on day one at GNR8. I then spent a full day in the field shadowing an electrician in order to build an AI voice assistant: call the bot, talk about the job, and it fills in time and activity reports automatically. The client paid for the MVP and for taking it forward and I handled handover to our senior dev team for integrations with their internal systems.

My role
Identified the problem independently on day one
Spent a full day in the field shadowing an electrician
Built a full-stack MVP in days, functional enough to demo to the client
Produced a trailer video with Selatek for internal sign-off
Handled handover to senior dev team for production integrations

Tekont

Real integration. Real companies. Real receipts.

Tekont connects directly to Bokio's accounting API. You photograph a receipt, the app reads it and suggests a booking, and you approve it. No manual data entry. Currently used by a small number of companies in the arts sector and actively being adopted by others.
